Pink Bubblegum Ice Cream

Bubblegum flavored ice cream loaded with candy-coated bubblegum pieces. It's like two treats in one!

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Bubble Gum [Sugar, Dextrose, Corn Syrup, Gum Base, Maltodextrin, Artificial Flavor, Resinous Glaze, Soy Lecithin (Emulsifier), Beeswax and Carnauba Wax, Red 3], Sugar, Corn Syrup, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80), Red 3, Red 40, Blue 1, Natural and Artificial Flavors.
