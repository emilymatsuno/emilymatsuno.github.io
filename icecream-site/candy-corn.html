Candy Corn Ice Cream

What's yellow, orange, and white all over? This delicious candy corn flavored mousse ice cream, that's what!

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Mousse Base [Partially Hydrogenated Palm Kernel Oil and Coconut Oil, Corn Syrup Solids, Sodium Caseinate (a Milk Protein), Propylene Glycol Monoester, Mono and Diglycerides, Cellulose Gum, Acetylated Monoglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan], Whey Powder, Yellow 6, Yellow 5, Red 40, Blue 1, Natural and Artificial Flavor.
