Chocolate Ice Cream

It is what it is. (And what it is is extra rich, creamy, mind-blowing chocolate ice cream)

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Chocolate Liquor and Cocoa processed with alkali, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80).
