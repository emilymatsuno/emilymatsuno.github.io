Creole Cream Cheese Ice Cream

This creamy, rich cream cheese flavored ice cream is velvety goodness with a Cajun flair. In other words, it's a 'Big Easy' treat.

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Cheesecake Base [Corn Syrup, Water, High Fructose Corn Syrup, Baker's Cheese Powder (Nonfat Milk, Rennet Casein, Sugar, Cream, Contains 2% or less of each of the following: Lactic Acid, Citric Acid, Sodium Hexametaphosphate, Dipotassium Phosphate, Silicon Dioxide), Buttermilk Powder, Lactic Acid, Natural Flavor (Contains Milk), Locust Bean Gum, Guar Gum, Sodium Benzoate (Preservative), Potassium Sorbate (Preservative)], Sugar, Corn Syrup, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80).
