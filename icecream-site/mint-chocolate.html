Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Chill out. A cool hit of mint flavored ice cream with the joy of chocolate chips throughout.

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips [Sugar, Chocolate Liquor, Chocolate Liquor processed with alkali, Milkfat (Butter Oil), Cocoa Butter, Soy Lecithin (Emulsifier)], Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80), Natural Flavor, Blue 1, Yellow 5.
