Peanut Butter 'N Banana Ice Cream

Go nuts for banana flavored ice cream! Then go bananas for a chunky peanut butter ribbon!

Ingredients
Cream, Peanut Butter Ribbon (Peanuts, Cottonseed and/or Peanut Oil, High Fructose Corn Syrup, Salt), Nonfat Milk, Bananas Foster Base [Banana Puree, Corn Syrup, Water, Propylene Glycol, Cinnamon, Natural Flavoring, Potassium Sorbate (Preservative), Ascorbic Acid (to help protect flavor), Sodium Benzoate (Preservative), Caramel Color], Sugar, Corn Syrup, Whey Powder, Vegetable Juice for Coloring (Pumpkin), Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80).
