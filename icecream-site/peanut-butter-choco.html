Peanut Butter 'N Chocolate Ice Cream

There's no better gift to your taste buds than chocolate ice cream tied with a chunky peanut butter ribbon.

Ingredients
Cream, Peanut Butter Ribbon (Peanuts, Cottonseed and/or Peanut Oil, High Fructose Corn Syrup, Salt), Nonfat Milk, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Chocolate Liquor and Cocoa processed with alkali, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80).
