Pralines 'N Cream Ice Cream

Taste some South in your mouth! Praline-coated pecan pieces and caramel in vanilla flavored ice cream.

Cream, Nonfat Milk, Caramel Ribbon [Corn Syrup, Sweetened Condensed Milk (Milk, Sugar), Water, High Fructose Corn Syrup, Butter (Sweet Cream, Salt), Propylene Glycol, Sodium Alginate, Salt, Natural and Artificial Flavors, Potassium Sorbate (Preservative), Soy Lecithin (Emulsifier), Baking Soda, Annatto Color], Praline Pecans [Sugar, Pecans, Butter (Cream, Salt), Corn Syrup, Salt], Sugar, Corn Syrup, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80), Natural and Artificial Flavor, Yellow 5, Yellow 6.
