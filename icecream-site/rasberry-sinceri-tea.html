Raspberry Sinceri-Tea

Honest-to-goodness sorbet made with Rainforest Alliance Certified™ black tea and a raspberry sorbet swirl.

Ingredients
Water, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Raspberry Puree (Red Raspberry Puree Concentrate, Sugar), Stabilizer Blend (Locust Bean Gum, Modified Cellulose, Guar Gum), Caramel Color, Black Tea Extract, Natural Flavor.
