Rocky Road Ice Cream

Chocolate ice cream. Heaps of miniature marshmallows. Almonds. So good you'll want to buy a house on this road.

Ingredients
Cream, Nonfat Milk, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Marshmallows (Corn Syrup, Sugar, Modified Cornstarch, Gelatin), Roasted Almonds (Almonds, Safflower Oil or Canola Oil), Chocolate Liquor and Cocoa processed with alkali, Whey Powder, Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Cellulose Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Guar Gum, Carrageenan, Polysorbate 80).
