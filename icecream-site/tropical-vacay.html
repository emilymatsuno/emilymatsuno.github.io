Tropical Vacation Frozen Yogurt

Give your taste buds a trip to the tropics. Pineapple and passion fruit flavored frozen yogurt and a passion fruit ribbon make for a perfectly delicious getaway. Made with live & active cultures.

Ingredients
Yogurt Base (Nonfat Milk, Cream, Yogurt Culture*), Passion Fruit Base (Corn Syrup, Passion Fruit Juice, Sugar, Water, High Fructose Corn Syrup, Modified Corn Starch, Sodium Citrate, Vanilla Extract), Nonfat Milk, Cream, Sugar, Corn Syrup, Pineapple Base [Pineapple, Sugar, Carrageenan, Citric Acid, Natural Flavor, Salt, Xanthan Gum, Cellulose Gum, Turmeric Color, Annatto Color, Sodium Benzoate (Preservative)], Stabilizer/Emulsifier Blend (Guar Gum, Mono and Diglycerides, Xanthan Gum, Polysorbate 80, Carrageenan), Vegetable Juice for Coloring (Pumpkin), Natural Flavor.*Contains Live Yogurt Cultures (Streptococcus Thermophilus, Lactobacillus delbrueckii subsp. Bulgaricus).
